# Canon

Locked facts for this campaign. Ground truth that all reasoning
must respect. Only the human adds or removes entries.

## World

- The Stolen Flame binds souls — it cannot be destroyed, only
  contained or transferred
- The Merchant Guild controls the eastern docks; the Thieves'
  Guild controls the western docks — they are in a cold war
- Veranthos has no functioning temple to Pharasma — the church
  was destroyed 40 years ago; this is a plot-relevant gap

## Characters

- Lord Caerith knows he is being used by the patron but
  believes he will eventually control the situation (he is
  wrong)
- Party reputation with the City Watch is currently Neutral
  (3/10)

## Tone

- Gritty political thriller with moments of horror, not
  heroic adventure
