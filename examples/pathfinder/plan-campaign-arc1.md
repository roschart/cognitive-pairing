# Plan: Campaign Arc 1 — The Stolen Flame

## Context

- Party is level 3, 4 players, meeting every 2 weeks
- Arc 1 centers on recovering a stolen artifact from a noble conspiracy
- The city of Veranthos is the base of operations
- Players have shown strong interest in political intrigue and mystery;
  less interest in dungeon crawls
- Previous session ended with the party discovering the Merchant Guild
  is involved

## Decisions

- **Artifact identity**: The Stolen Flame is a soul-bound relic, not a
  weapon — limits its use as a combat MacGuffin
- **Main antagonist**: Lord Caerith is the face, not the mastermind —
  there is a hidden patron behind the conspiracy
- **Tone**: Gritty political thriller with moments of horror, not
  heroic adventure
